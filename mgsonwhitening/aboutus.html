ALL ABOUT AGELESS BEAUTY

AFFORDABLE, GORGEOUS AND BEAUTY WHICH YOU DESERVE!

Ageless Beauty is the newest beauty product in the Philippines. This aims to help Filipinos with their acne problems. It is affordable, natural, safe, and proven formulated offered by MGSON Whitening and Beauty Shop.

Mary Grace Manabit is an entrepreneur who is married to Jerson Manabit, blessed with two beautiful daughters Maryson and Mary Elisha Manabit. She reveals how she was able to transition from her career as a Jollibee manager way back in 2011 to 2015 and later engaged in business.

They began by selling eggs, rice, oil, and other foodstuffs. Unfortunately, they got bankrupt. Her desperation to escape financial deprivation prompted her to start another business, which she eventually became successful at. Then, they met a friend with whom they have witnessed his acne breakout problem. To their surprise, there were no traces of breakout on her face. It was then a beauty product introduced to them.

In the year 2019 of February, Ageless Beauty's rejuvenating set was born. It's introduced to the public by selling only three sets, then six sets, twenty sets, one hundred sets, and even thousands of sets. This rejuvenating set has a kojic acid soap to help lighten visible sun damage and reduce the production of melanin use in skin lightening. A rejuvenating cream that gives stability to the skin to remain young. A rejuvenating toner removes impurities from the skin. And baby cream SPF45 targets wrinkles, fine lines, and age spots to reveal younger-looking skin and radiant complexion.

MGSON Whitening and Beauty Shop successfully launched their own brand "Ageless Beauty". Registered in the Department of Trade Industry (DTI) on October 12, 2019, License to Operate (LTO) as cosmetic traders on July 2020, and as a cosmetic manufacturer in November 2019. Also, it is approved by the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) in 2019.

Because of the good feedback from their consumers, users, and resellers. And, gaining stable success and consistently high demand, the MGSON whitening and beauty shop expanded and created other beauty products for daily use. Like, body care, oils, lip care, hair care, her & his scent, and even janitorial products.

Indeed, it was a wise choice. Mary Grace Manabit said that "She believes there is light at the end of the tunnel if only you never give up".
